Priya's current reality
Before — WhatsApp chaos
Every order is a long back-and-forth. "What size?" "Which flavor?" "What date?" "Any message on the cake?" — typed fresh every time. Orders get mixed up. Details get forgotten.
After — Google Form
Customer fills one form. All details captured instantly. Priya opens a Google Sheet and sees every order neatly listed. No back-and-forth. No confusion. No missed orders.
What a customer used to send Priya
Hi I want to order a cake for my son's birthday on the 15th. Chocolate flavor. Can you do fondant? Not sure what size. Budget around 1500.
Oh and can you write Happy Birthday Arjun on it
Also what's your delivery charge for Bandra?
Teaching point
Three separate messages, missing half the details Priya needs — no delivery address, no phone number. She spends 20 more minutes chasing information before confirming the order. Multiply this by 10 orders a week.
Live prompt to run in class

"Create a Google Form for a home bakery called Priya's Cakes in Mumbai. Write clear friendly labels for each field and add helpful placeholder text. Also write a warm confirmation message the customer sees after submitting."

Building it in Google Forms — step by step
1
Open Google Forms
Go to forms.google.com — free with any Google account. Click the + button to start a blank form.
forms.google.com → New form
2
Add the title and description
Paste the AI-generated title and subtitle exactly. This is what the customer sees first — it sets the tone and builds trust.
3
Add each field from the AI output
For text answers use Short Answer. For flavor and size use Multiple Choice. Mark important fields as Required so customers can't skip them.
Add question → choose field type → toggle Required
4
Set the confirmation message
Paste the AI-written thank you message here — warm, reassuring, tells the customer what happens next. They see this immediately after submitting.
Settings → Presentation → Confirmation message
5
Connect to Google Sheets
Every submission automatically lands in a spreadsheet. Priya opens the sheet each morning and sees all orders in one place — no digging through WhatsApp chats.
Responses tab → Sheets icon → Create new sheet
6
Share the form link everywhere
Copy the link and add it to Priya's WhatsApp Business profile, Instagram bio, and pin it as a WhatsApp status. Every enquiry now goes straight to the form.
Send → Copy link
